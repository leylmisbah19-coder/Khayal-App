About this app

Two real AI calls happen when you click "Style me." First, a vision model looks at your photo, age, and measurements and invents 3 outfit concepts suited to current trends — it's instructed never to comment on your body, only on proportions relevant to fit, the way a tailor would. Second, an image-editing model takes your actual uploaded photo and edits the clothing onto it directly, keeping your face and pose — that's the "doll" preview, except it's really you. Because Dubai malls don't expose live public stock APIs, each outfit links out to a real, live search on Namshi, Zara UAE, Ounass, H&M UAE, and SHEIN UAE so you can check current price and stock yourself. Your photo and API key never leave your computer except straight to OpenAI, via the local server you're running.
